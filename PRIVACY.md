# Onyx Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 14, 2026

Onyx is a local-first Windows media player. It does not operate an account service, advertising network, analytics service, telemetry service, or cloud backend.

## Data Onyx handles

Onyx processes information that you provide so it can load and play media, including local playlist files, remote playlist URLs, Xtream connection details, XMLTV guide URLs, favorites, recent channels, playback state, and application settings.

## Where data is stored

Application settings, credential-free source metadata, guide caches, favorites, recents, and playback state are stored locally on your Windows device. Xtream passwords and remote playlist and guide URLs are stored separately in Windows Credential Manager.

Onyx does not send this information to the developer. When you configure a remote playlist, guide, stream, or Xtream service, Onyx connects directly from your device to the address or service you supplied. Those third-party services process requests under their own terms and privacy policies.

## Data sharing

Onyx does not sell, rent, share, or use personal information for advertising. It has no telemetry or developer-operated collection endpoint.

## Data removal

You can remove saved sources and related credentials from within Onyx. Uninstalling Onyx removes its packaged application data according to Windows behavior. You can also remove saved Onyx credentials through Windows Credential Manager.

## Children

Onyx is a general-purpose media player and is not directed to children. It does not provide or curate media content.

## Changes

Material changes to this policy will be published in this repository with an updated effective date.
